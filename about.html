Evoghen was founded on a straightforward belief: federal agencies deserve technology partners who have actually done the work — not just managed it from a distance.

Our Story

Why We Exist

Federal technology modernization is at an inflection point. Agencies are under pressure to adopt AI, migrate to the cloud, and maintain rigorous compliance — all at the same time, often with constrained resources and limited internal capacity.

Evoghen was created to fill that gap. We're an Arizona-based small business that brings practitioner-level expertise across four of the most in-demand federal technology domains: AI modernization, EdTech compliance, Microsoft 365 and Azure cloud, and technical business analysis.

Our name reflects our purpose. Evo — from the Latin for evolution, continuous forward movement. Gen — from genesis, generation, the act of creating something new. Together: evolving systems through intelligent generation of solutions.

Our Approach

How We Work

We don't believe in delivering solutions and moving on. Our model is built around embedded expertise — working alongside agency program offices and prime contractor teams as a genuine extension of your capability, not a vendor on the outside looking in.

  • Compliance embedded from day one. We design solutions with Title IV, FedRAMP, NIST, and FERPA requirements built in — never bolted on after the fact.
  • Outcomes over deliverables. We define success by agency mission advancement, not page counts or slide decks.
  • Honest scoping. We only take engagements where we can deliver genuine value. If a problem falls outside our expertise, we'll tell you — and help you find the right partner.
  • Small team, full accountability. As a small business, you work directly with our principal — no hand-offs to junior staff after the contract is signed.
