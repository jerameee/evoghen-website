Core Service Areas

Four areas.
One integrated team.

Evoghen delivers across four high-demand federal technology domains. Whether you need a subcontractor to fill a capability gap or a consulting partner for a full program office engagement, we bring depth — not just bandwidth.

Service Area 01

AI & Digital Modernization

Federal AI isn't just about adopting the latest model — it's about building systems that are explainable, compliant, and actually useful to the humans relying on them. Evoghen specializes in taking AI from strategy to production in federal environments where the stakes are real and the constraints are complex.

Our principal's published work on context engineering and agentic workflow design is a direct extension of our delivery approach — rigorous, tested, and grounded in how large language models actually behave in production.

What We Deliver

  • RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline design and implementation
  • Agentic AI workflow architecture for federal use cases
  • Context engineering strategy and prompt infrastructure
  • AI readiness assessments and modernization roadmaps
  • LLM integration with existing agency systems and data sources
  • Responsible AI governance frameworks aligned to OMB AI policy

Best fit for: Agencies piloting or scaling AI programs, prime contractors needing AI subcontract support, program offices seeking AI strategy without vendor lock-in.

Service Area 02

EdTech & Title IV Compliance

Title IV federal student aid compliance is one of the most technically demanding regulatory environments in the federal space. The rules are dense, the systems are complex, and the consequences of getting it wrong are serious. Evoghen brings deep, firsthand expertise in this domain — not surface-level familiarity.

We've worked inside these systems. We understand how Title IV intersects with technology, how FSA expects institutions and vendors to demonstrate compliance, and where the common failure points are.

What We Deliver

  • Title IV regulatory compliance review and gap analysis
  • FERPA compliance advisory for EdTech vendors and institutions
  • FSA systems analysis and program office support
  • Student aid technology modernization planning
  • Compliance training and stakeholder briefings
  • Policy implementation support for Department of Education engagements

Best fit for: Department of Education, FSA, state education agencies, EdTech companies navigating federal compliance requirements.

Service Area 03

Microsoft 365 & Azure Cloud

The Microsoft ecosystem is the backbone of most federal agency IT environments — and getting it right requires more than certification. It requires understanding how M365, Azure, Entra ID, and SharePoint interact in real federal tenants, under real security constraints, with real users who need to get real work done.

Evoghen delivers full-lifecycle Microsoft cloud services with a security-first, governance-aware approach aligned to FedRAMP and NIST standards.

What We Deliver

  • Microsoft 365 tenant administration and optimization
  • Azure infrastructure design and cloud migration support
  • Entra ID (Azure AD) identity and access management
  • SharePoint architecture, governance, and site design
  • FedRAMP alignment and NIST 800-53 control mapping
  • Microsoft Purview compliance and data governance

Best fit for: Civilian agencies on M365 GCC or GCC High, program offices migrating to Azure, prime contractors needing cloud subcontract support.

Service Area 04

Technical Business Analysis

Great technology fails without clear requirements, aligned stakeholders, and disciplined execution. Evoghen provides embedded technical BA support that bridges the gap between agency program offices and the technical teams building on their behalf.

We speak both languages — the policy and mission language of federal program managers, and the systems and architecture language of the engineers doing the work. That translation capability is rare, and it's where we add the most value.

What We Deliver

  • Requirements elicitation, documentation, and management
  • Business process analysis and improvement mapping
  • Agile product ownership and backlog management
  • Stakeholder management and facilitation
  • User story writing and acceptance criteria development
  • Program office support for civilian agency modernization initiatives

Best fit for: Program offices launching modernization projects, prime contractors needing BA subcontract support, agencies transitioning to Agile delivery models.
